Impact Generated Moonquakes

Meteor impacts can also cause moonquakes, and since there is no atmosphere on the moon, every small meteorite that is headed for the moon will strike it.

These impacts cause rippling earthquakes that can be detected by seismometers.

Around 13% of all identified events,the second largest group of lunar seismic events, are generated by impacts, both of natural meteoroids and of artificial sources

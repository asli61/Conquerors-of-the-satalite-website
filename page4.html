Shallow Moonquakes

These are the most powerful and the most worrisome quakes for researchers and those eager to colonize the moon. Of the four types of quakes, these are the ones that could do some real damage.

Some of these quakes can be fairly strong, around five on the Richter scale(NASA , 2019). Furthermore, shallow moonquakes lasted a remarkably long time. Once they got going, all continued more than 10 minutes(NASA , 2006)

The exact cause of these shallow quakes is unknown, but analysis gives the first evidence that these faults are still active and likely producing moonquakes today.
